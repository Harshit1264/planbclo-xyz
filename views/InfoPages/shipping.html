Shipping Policy

At PLAN B CLO, we aim to deliver your products in the fastest and most efficient way possible.

Shipping Methods and Delivery Times

We offer standard and express shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 working days
  • Express Shipping: 1-3 working days

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on the weight of the package and the delivery location. The exact shipping charges will be displayed at checkout.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a notification once your order has been shipped.

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. We are working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment.
